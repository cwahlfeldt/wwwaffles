HOW TO PLAY

Threes! meets a tactical dungeon. Everything on the board moves together — and nothing is ever simply erased.

CONTROLS

  • Swipe on touch, or arrow keys / WASD on desktop
  • Every swipe slides every tile in that direction until blocked
  • A new tile spawns each turn — check the NEXT preview above the board

CORE PRINCIPLE: PRESSURE

Every swipe adds a new tile. Nothing ever "clears" without intent — you have to engineer collisions to free space. Tension builds as the board fills.

COMBAT

Sliding into an enemy attacks it — you do not move.
If the enemy dies, its cell empties.
If it survives and the tile behind it is open, it gets shoved back.
If that tile is blocked (wall or another tile), the enemy counter-attacks — this is the only way enemies damage you.

UNITS

  • Hero (you)
  • Rat — 1 HP, 1 ATK
  • Goblin — 2 HP, 1 ATK
  • Orc — 3 HP, 2 ATK
  • Skeleton — 4 HP, 2 ATK
  • Demon — 5 HP, 3 ATK

Two same-tier enemies sliding together merge into the next tier up — Threes!-style. A threat spike, but fewer pieces on the board.

TRAPS

  • Spike Trap
    Slides like everything else.

Enemy + Trap → enemy takes trap damage, trap is destroyed. Chain enemies into traps to clear them cheaply.
Trap + Trap → both break.
You + Trap → you take damage, trap is destroyed.

LOOT

  • Heart — restores 3 HP
  • Sword — +1 ATK permanently
  • Gold — +5 score

Loot also slides. Enemies and traps will destroy it if they reach it first.

GAME OVER

  • HP drops to 0, or
  • the board fills and no swipe produces a collision or movement
